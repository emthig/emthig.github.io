Dr. Susan La Flesche Picotte

The first Native American woman to receive a medical degree in the United States

A Timeline of Dr. La Flesche Picotte's Life

  • 1865 - Born on the Omaha Reservation in Nebraska
  • 1880 - Attended the Elizabeth Institute in Elizabeth, New Jersey
  • 1882 - Returned to the reservation to teach
  • 1884 - Studied at the Hampton Institute in Hampton, Virginia
  • 1886 - Was accepted at the Woman's Medical College of Pennsylvania
  • 1889 - Graduated at the top of her class & returned to be a physician on the Omaha reservation
  • 1890s - Campaigned against alcohol
  • 1913 - The reservation hospital she had campaigned for was completed and named after her
  • 1915 - Died of bone cancer
